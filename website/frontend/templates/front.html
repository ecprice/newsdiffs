Keeping Track of What Trump's Changing

Track Trump's Changes keeps track of changes made to government websites.

We know Donald Trump plays fast and loose with his words (even if they're the best ones). Using the open source code from NewsDiffs, we're keeping track of what changes on a number of .gov sites.

Since Donald Trump took office, climate change pages have disappeared, LGBT protections pages have vanished, and misleading stats about our cities' murder rates have been quietly updated. Track Trump's Changes wants to give citizens the power to see what's changing in real-time.
